Europe PMC is one of the worldâ€™s largest biomedical literature repositories. Our mission is "to build open, full-text scientific literature resources and support innovation by engaging users, enabling contributors, and integrating related research data." The Europe PMC team works on developing innovative solutions for literature research and analysis.

Europe PMC offers free access to publications and related information for developers to build new applications on top of open-access content. Europe PMC provides RESTful APIs to access publications, grants awarded by the Europe PMC Funders, and annotations text-mined from abstracts and open-access, full-text articles.

The Europe PMC techblog is a space where we share information about our APIs, how to use our resources to create services, and other technical things we've learned of general use.

If you would like to learn more about what Europe PMC has to offer, you can visit our organisation page on GitHub, have a look at Europe PMC's developer resources, or join the Europe PMC developers' forum.
